## 1. Summary

LLV's intellectual property is brand and process, not patents. Its defensibility comes from a trusted, premium brand, a documented operating playbook, and an integrated technology platform.

## 2. Trademark

The "Luxury Lifestyle Vault" wordmark and the tagline "Your Lifestyle, Wherever Life Takes You." Trademark registration is planned; an application for the wordmark and tagline is to be filed with the USPTO. Securing the mark protects the brand across marketing and the membership experience.

## 3. Brand System

A complete brand and design system — the gold-framed vault mark, the house color palette, and a master document standard — gives every member and investor touchpoint a consistent, premium identity. The brand book lives in the asset library.

## 4. Domain & Digital Assets

The domain luxurylifestylevault.com is owned. The member and investor platform is a proprietary codebase maintained in a private repository.

## 5. Process IP

The documented intake, custody, cleaning, seasonal rotation, and delivery playbook, together with the Trust & Liability framework, is the operational know-how that makes the service repeatable and insurable.

## 6. Third-Party IP & Guardrails

Software relies on standard licensed and open-source components that LLV has the right to use; the business does not depend on third-party IP it does not license. Per the guardrails, LLV documents custody and records owner-provided values — it never authenticates or appraises items.
