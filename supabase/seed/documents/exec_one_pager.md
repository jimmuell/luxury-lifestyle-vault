Executive One-Pager

Luxury Wardrobe Logistics & Asset Management for Affluent, Multi-Residence Living

LLV transforms a scattered, at-risk luxury wardrobe into a fully documented, professionally managed wardrobe.

**What we do.** LLV helps affluent individuals with multiple residences store, protect, move, manage, and ultimately monetize the high-value wardrobes they own — designer apparel, handbags, watches, and collectible fashion — through a single, trusted, concierge-driven platform. LLV owns the custody layer: intake, documentation, climate-controlled storage, chain-of-custody, condition reporting, and digital cataloging.

**The problem.** Affluent households increasingly live across two or more residences with wardrobes worth tens or hundreds of thousands of dollars, yet have no trusted system to manage those assets across locations. Items are packed and shipped by hand, risk damage in transit, and carry no record behind them.

**The solution.** Three membership tiers — (1) Seasonal Wardrobe Rotation: recurring clean, store, and rotate between residences; (2) Total Wardrobe Management: arrive to a fully prepared closet, cleaned and organized; (3) On-Demand Occasion Fulfillment: browse your own photographed wardrobe and request specific items shipped for any event. No competitor offers Tier 3 — LLV's signature differentiator.

**The moat.** Every item held becomes an entry in a proprietary system of record — ownership, condition, and location over time. That documented record, not the storage, is the asset: it compounds into a defensible intelligence layer, deep recurring affluent relationships, and a documented low-loss-rate custody operation, and can in time support insurance, estate planning, lending, and resale.

**Market validation.** The RealReal — the largest authenticated luxury-resale marketplace — posted roughly $606M in Q1 2026 GMV (+24% YoY) and 1.08M active buyers, proving affluent consumers will ship and trust third parties with luxury goods. LLV occupies the ownership and stewardship stage that comes before resale, and is built to feed The RealReal both supply and demand — complementary, not competitive.

**Business model.** Recurring by design: Tier 1 and Tier 2 subscriptions, Tier 3 per-request fees, a protection membership, and RealReal referral. Asset-light where it should be (cleaning and transport are rented from premium providers) and asset-owning where it matters (custody and the system of record).

**The pilot.** Launches October 2026, the start of snowbird season, with 10–15 founding members in a Wisconsin–Arizona corridor on a $25K–$40K budget — proving recurring revenue, retention, and a low-loss-rate operation before expanding to additional corridors.

**Leadership & platform.** Founded by a technology entrepreneur who previously built and sold a company to UnitedHealthcare. The platform — client, provider, and admin apps with a digital wardrobe catalog, AI-assisted search, and payments — is already built and in testing ahead of launch.

**Vision.** LLV begins as luxury wardrobe logistics and asset management and grows into the trusted operating platform for luxury asset ownership — extending from wardrobe into handbags, watches, jewelry, collectibles, and estate transitions.
