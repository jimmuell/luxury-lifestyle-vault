## 1. Summary

LLV's legal and regulatory posture as of June 2026, pre-launch. Several items are open launch gates and are tracked on the launch board. This is a status summary, not legal advice; counsel review is pending.

## 2. Entity

Wisconsin LLC and EIN are pending as a launch gate, with Arizona foreign registration to follow. The entity name and address feed the live Terms and Privacy pages once formed.

## 3. Insurance

Bailee and general-liability coverage is being bound. Bailee (warehouse legal liability) insures client items in LLV's care, custody, and control and in transit, up to set limits; the member is protected through the service agreement.

## 4. Member Agreements

Terms of Service and a Privacy Policy are live on luxurylifestylevault.com. The member service agreement defines liability, the declared-value mechanism, and claim and inspection windows (final caps to align with the bound insurance).

## 5. Data & Security

Member and investor data is encrypted at rest and in transit and hosted on Supabase (SOC 2). Row-level security enforces access at the database layer, documents are served via signed, expiring URLs, and the investor room is NDA-gated.

## 6. Communications

SMS messaging uses A2P 10DLC registration, pending verification, with member opt-in and consent captured and recorded.

## 7. Open Items

The WI LLC and EIN, the bailee and general-liability binding, and A2P verification are the open compliance gates, each tracked on the launch board. None changes the substance of the model; they are sequencing steps before public launch.
