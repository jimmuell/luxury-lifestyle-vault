## 1. Overview

Luxury Lifestyle Vault is founder-led with a lean operating team spanning the Wisconsin–Arizona corridor. The structure is intentionally small for the founding-member pilot, with a clear hiring plan tied to the raise.

## 2. Founder & CEO

**Jim Mueller — Founder & CEO.** Leads vision, product, partnerships, and fundraising. A technology entrepreneur, he previously built and sold a company to UnitedHealthcare. He founded LLV after recognizing that affluent clients with wardrobes spread across multiple seasonal residences lack a trusted, insured service to store, rotate, clean, and manage them.

## 3. Operations (Arizona)

A family operator manages the Arizona side, running the Scottsdale pilot, member intake, custody handling, and corridor logistics.

## 4. Org Chart

A lean founding structure: Founder / CEO oversees three areas — Operations (Arizona pilot, intake, logistics), the Platform (the member and investor software), and Provider Partners (professional cleaning and resale). Member-facing concierge support reports into Operations.

## 5. Advisors & Board

No advisory board or board of directors is in place today. LLV plans to recruit advisors across luxury retail, insurance and bailee coverage, and logistics following the raise, and expects to add an investor board seat at the priced round. Board profiles will surface to the board tier of the portal as they are confirmed.

## 6. Hiring Plan

Near-term roles after the raise: an operations / member-success lead and additional corridor logistics capacity as the pilot scales beyond founding members.
