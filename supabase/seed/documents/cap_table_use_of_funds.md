## 1. Summary

This page summarizes LLV's capitalization today and the terms of the round being raised. LLV is wholly founder-owned and is raising its first outside capital. The round terms and figures below are illustrative planning numbers, not a priced valuation or an offer of securities; they are subject to founder confirmation and counsel review, and the valuation cap is set in negotiation with the lead investor. This is not legal, tax, or investment advice.

## 2. Current Capitalization (Pre-Raise)

**LLV is 100% founder-owned.** Jim Mueller holds the entire company as sole founder. There are no other equity holders, and no prior investors, SAFEs, convertible notes, or other instruments are outstanding. No employee option pool has been created yet. Because the company has not completed a priced round, no share count has been issued; ownership is expressed in percentages.

## 3. The Round

LLV is raising $500,000 as its seed round. The proposed instrument is a post-money SAFE, which defers pricing to the next priced round — the appropriate structure when there is not yet a market comparable. The working valuation cap is $3.5 million (post-money), with no separate discount; that cap implies new investors convert to roughly 14% of the company. A discount of 10–20% could be added to reward early commitments. The cap and final terms are subject to negotiation with the lead investor and must be papered by counsel.

## 4. Illustrative Ownership After the Round

On conversion of the $500,000 SAFE at a $3.5 million post-money cap, new investors hold approximately 14.3% and the founder approximately 85.7%, before any option pool. LLV expects to create an employee option pool of roughly 10% at the priced round, which would dilute all holders pro rata at that time. These percentages are illustrative and will change with the final cap, the amount actually raised, and the priced-round terms.

## 5. Use of Funds

The $500,000 is allocated to operations and hiring, 45% ($225,000); pilot and corridor launch, 15% ($75,000); platform and technology, 13% ($65,000); insurance and legal/compliance, 12% ($60,000); and marketing and contingency, 15% ($75,000). The round is sized to fund roughly 18–24 months of runway — through the founding-member pilot and expansion toward three to four corridors, with the founder transitioning off sweat equity.

## 6. Financial Basis

The raise is sized bottom-up from the milestone and runway, not derived from a multi-year revenue forecast. Supporting figures come from the LLV Financial Model (folder 03): a Year-1 pilot of about 12 members and roughly $55.7K of revenue, scaling illustratively to about $235K in Year 2 and about $612K in Year 3. The Year-2 and Year-3 figures are directional planning estimates, not formula-derived commitments.

## 7. Notes & Caveats

No securities are offered by this document. All figures are illustrative and pre-decisional: no shares have been issued, the SAFE is unpriced, and the valuation cap, discount, and option pool are subject to negotiation and counsel review. A detailed working capitalization spreadsheet will be maintained alongside the priced round. The source of record for the financial figures is the LLV Financial Model (folder 03).
