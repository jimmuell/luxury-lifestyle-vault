## Candidate profiles

### 1. Martinizing Cleaners — *lead candidate*
Family-owned and operated for 50+ years, serving Southeast Wisconsin. Marketed explicitly toward designer/couture clients ("the same attention to detail you give in picking out an Armani suit"). Garment care spans **wedding-gown preservation, couture cleaning, fur and leather cleaning**, area-rug cleaning, and **alterations / repair / restoration**. Offers **free pickup and delivery**.
- **Why it fits:** Broadest premium service set of any WI candidate; the fur + leather + couture + restoration combination is exactly what the LLV model needs at the WI end, and pickup/delivery already exists.
- **To confirm on the call:** weekly capacity, B2B/wholesale or volume terms, bailee/in-transit insurance posture, turnaround times, willingness to coordinate scheduled corridor handoffs (not just retail drop-off), handling of high-declared-value items.

### 2. Klinke Cleaners — *local convenience / overflow*
14 locations across the Madison and Milwaukee areas, including **Brookfield** (West Capitol Drive, just west of Lilly Road — close to the founder). Full service "from shirts to wedding gowns, leather to linens," with archival-quality **wedding-gown preservation**.
- **Why it fits:** Proximity + footprint make it ideal for fast, reliable, high-volume seasonal-rotation work; a strong secondary/overflow partner and a convenient physical handoff point on the WI side.
- **To confirm:** couture/designer handling depth (vs. volume cleaning), pickup/delivery availability, partnership terms, insurance.

### 3. The London Cleaners — *premium couture/leather specialist*
Consistently ranked among Milwaukee's top-3 cleaners (50-point inspection lists). Known for handling **delicate projects — leathers and special fabrics** — plus difficult stain removal and tailoring repairs.
- **Why it fits:** A boutique specialist for the highest-value couture and leather pieces, complementing a higher-volume primary.
- **To confirm:** scale/capacity, pickup/delivery, terms, insurance.

### 4. Prestige Cleaners — *alternate*
30 years in business; dedicated **seamstresses and tailors**, leather work, and a reputation for preserving wedding gowns, christening gowns, and family heirlooms. Located at 4419 W Fond Du Lac Ave (Sunset Heights, Milwaukee).
- **Considerations:** Some reviews flag **higher pricing** vs. competitors — not disqualifying for a luxury positioning, but confirm value and consistency. Inner-city Milwaukee location is further from Brookfield than Martinizing/Klinke.

### 5. Redi-Quick Dry Cleaners — *backup*
Top-3 rated, family-owned ~30 yrs, heirloom/gown preservation with modern, eco-conscious processes at competitive rates. A solid lower-cost backup if a premium partner falls through.

## Note: luxury handbag / leather-goods restoration (Tier 4 expansion)
For the eventual **luxury asset management** phase (handbags, watches, leather goods — Expansion Phase 4), local Milwaukee handbag-restoration depth is thin. The recognized standard for designer handbag/leather restoration is **national mail-in specialists** — e.g., **Margaret's Cleaners** (since 1953), **Leather Surgeons** (Chanel/Hermès, 30 yrs), **Rago Brothers**, **ReLeather**. Recommendation: keep WI garment-care (Martinizing/Klinke) for the seasonal-rotation pilot, and partner a national handbag/leather specialist separately when Tier 4 launches rather than expecting a local dry cleaner to cover it.
