## 2. Discovery call script

**Opening (30 sec):** "Thanks for taking a few minutes. Quick version: LLV manages seasonal wardrobes for affluent families moving between Wisconsin and Scottsdale. We coordinate everything; the actual cleaning and finishing we hand to a couple of top partners at each end. I'd like to learn about your shop and see if there's a fit — then I'll tell you exactly what working together would look like."

**Discovery — confirm the partnership basics (the things the research couldn't):**
1. **Capacity** — "Roughly how many garments a week can you turn around comfortably in your busy season? Could you absorb a batch of 20–40 pieces on a scheduled date?"
2. **Service depth** — "Walk me through what you handle in-house vs. send out: couture/delicate, **fur, leather/suede, handbags**, wedding-gown preservation, alterations/repair?"
3. **Turnaround** — "Standard turnaround? Do you offer a rush option, and at what premium?"
4. **Pickup / delivery** — "Do you currently do pickup and delivery? Could you coordinate **scheduled** corridor handoffs rather than walk-in drop-off?"
5. **High-value handling** — "How do you handle high-declared-value items — say a $5,000+ gown or a designer leather piece? Any per-item value limits?"
6. **Insurance** — "What's your coverage for items in your care (bailee/loss/damage)? We carry our own in-transit coverage and want to align on the handoff."
7. **Terms** — "We'd operate as a standard vendor at your normal rates initially — no revenue share, no exclusivity asked at pilot. Is there a volume or B2B account structure you offer?"
8. **Logistics contact** — "Who would be our day-to-day point of contact, and how do you prefer to receive scheduled jobs?"

**Close:** "This is incredibly helpful. Based on what you've described, [you sound like a strong fit / I think there's a real fit]. Our soft launch is October — can I send a one-page summary and follow up to firm up a simple agreement? And could I do a brief in-person visit to see the operation?"

**Red/green flags to listen for:**
- 🟢 In-house fur + leather + couture; existing pickup/delivery; comfortable with high-value pieces; clear point of contact.
- 🟡 Sends couture/leather out (adds a hop + risk); no pickup/delivery; vague on insurance.
- 🔴 No capacity in peak season; uncomfortable with high-declared-value custody; unwilling to do scheduled handoffs.

**Who we are.** Luxury Lifestyle Vault (LLV) is a premium managed-logistics platform for affluent, mobile clients. **What we're proposing.** A simple **vendor partnership**: LLV brings you **recurring, scheduled, high-value garment-care work**, and you provide your expert cleaning, finishing, and preservation at your standard rates. **Terms at pilot.**

**Why partner with LLV.**
- A **new, recurring revenue channel** with no marketing spend on your part.
- **Premium, well-handled work** — high-value garments, organized batches, clear expectations.
- Association with a **luxury concierge brand** serving an affluent clientele.
