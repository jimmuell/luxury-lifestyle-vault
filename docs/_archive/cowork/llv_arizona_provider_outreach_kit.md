## 2. Discovery call script

**Opening (30 sec):** "Thanks for the time. Quick version: LLV manages seasonal wardrobes for affluent families moving between Wisconsin and Scottsdale. We coordinate everything; the cleaning and finishing we hand to a couple of top partners at each end. I'd like to learn about your operation and see if there's a fit — then I'll tell you exactly what working together would look like. Our Arizona side is managed locally by [daughter's name]."

**Discovery — confirm the partnership basics:**
1. **Capacity** — "Roughly how many garments a week can you turn around comfortably in season? Could you absorb a scheduled batch of 20–40 pieces?"
2. **Service depth** — "What's in-house vs. sent out: couture/delicate, **fur, leather/suede, handbags**, gown preservation, alterations/repair?" *(RAVE: confirm handbag/accessory depth and clean-by-mail intake.)*
3. **Turnaround** — "Standard turnaround? Rush option and premium?"
4. **Pickup / delivery** — "Do you do pickup/delivery, and can you coordinate **scheduled** corridor handoffs vs. walk-in?" *(European Couture: confirm concierge pickup footprint across Scottsdale/Paradise Valley.)*
5. **High-value handling** — "How do you handle $5,000+ gowns or designer leather? Any per-item value limits?"
6. **Insurance** — "Coverage for items in your care (bailee/loss/damage)? We carry in-transit coverage and want to align on the handoff."
7. **Terms** — "We'd operate as a standard vendor at your normal rates initially — no revenue share, no exclusivity at pilot. Any volume/B2B account structure?"
8. **Logistics contact** — "Who's our day-to-day contact, and how do you prefer to receive scheduled jobs?"

**Close:** "Really helpful. Based on this, [strong fit / real fit]. Our soft launch is October — can I send a one-page summary and have [daughter's name] follow up to firm up a simple agreement and arrange a brief visit?"

**Red/green flags:**
- 🟢 In-house handbag/leather + couture; clean-by-mail or concierge pickup; comfortable with high-value custody; clear contact.
- 🟡 Sends couture/leather out; no pickup/delivery; vague on insurance.
- 🔴 No peak-season capacity; uncomfortable with high-declared-value items; won't do scheduled handoffs.

- A short, plain-language services agreement — not a complex contract.

**Why partner with LLV.**
- A **new, recurring revenue channel** with no marketing spend on your part.
- **Premium, well-handled work** — high-value garments, organized batches, clear expectations, a local AZ point of contact.
- Association with a **luxury concierge brand** serving an affluent snowbird clientele.
