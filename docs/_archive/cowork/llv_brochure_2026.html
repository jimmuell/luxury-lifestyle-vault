A managed concierge platform that stores, cares for, moves, and protects luxury wardrobes for affluent households living across more than one home.

The Opportunity

Affluent households increasingly live across two or more residences and own wardrobes worth tens or hundreds of thousands of dollars — designer apparel, handbags, watches, fine accessories. Yet there is no trusted way to store, move, and manage those assets across homes. Seasonal moves are done by hand: slow, stressful, and risky. Pieces are forgotten, damaged, or never worn — and there is no simple path to protect or eventually monetize them.

The Concept

Luxury Lifestyle Vault is the concierge layer for that entire lifecycle. We take custody of a client's wardrobe — cataloged, photographed, climate-controlled, and insured — and handle everything around it: seasonal rotation between residences, white-glove delivery, and on-demand fulfillment of any specific piece for any occasion, anywhere. We own the custody and the digital record; we partner with vetted premium providers for cleaning and transport.

Arrive at every home to a wardrobe that is already cleaned, pressed, organized — and waiting.
